Liu's Bacon Cheeseburgers

Description

A bacon cheeseburger is a true classic, best eaten every single day.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bun
  • 1 Patty (100g)
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • 2 strips of bacon
  • 1 tsp of chili mayo
  • 1 tsp of spicy bbq sauce

Steps

  1. Bake the bacon in the oven at 220Â°C for about 15 minutes.
  2. Fry the patty in a pan at medium heat for about 6-8 minutes depending on thickness.
  3. Microwave the bun for 45 seconds or put it in a toaster.
  4. Stack everything on top of each other and enjoy the taste of Liu's Bacon Cheeseburger.
